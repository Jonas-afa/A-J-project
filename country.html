Admission

Admissions are by application only. International students must show English proficiency. To do so, they have to take the IELTS or the TOEFL. Study Link Uganda holds various partnerships with institutions providing intensive preparation to both these exams.

Tuition Fees

Tuition fees in many countries are roughly equivalent to tuition fees in the UK. Some public institutions only charge around 5,000€ per year while others can charge up to 15,000€. Community colleges are a great way to save money. Students can complete their first two years of study in a community college where tuition fees are usually low before transferring to a prestigious university.
